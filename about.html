About Colbenson Carpentry

Founded and run by a father and son, Colbenson Carpentry takes pride in making sure we care about our customer and make sure they are satisfied. We love doing our job and making our customer's home a better place

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering exceptional carpentry services, ensuring that each project is tailored to meet the needs and vision of our clients. From custom tables to complete home remodels, our goal is to enhance the beauty and functionality of your space with precision and attention to detail.

Our Services

  • Custom Woodworking: Custom furniture, cabinets, shelving, fireplace, mantel and more
  • Home Remodeling: Home renovations, kitchens, bathrooms, and more
  • Wood Stain Identify wood stain and remove it
  • Repairs and Restorations: Wood repairs, refinishing, and restoration services

Our Values

At Colbenson Carpentry, we believe in honesty, quality, and reliability. Our customers are our priority, and we always strive to exceed expectations by delivering on time, within budget, and with outstanding craftsmanship.
